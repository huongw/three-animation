Earth

Spin me

  • In the vast expanse of endless space, A gem of blue, a wondrous place, A world of life and beauty rare, Our home, the Earth, suspended there.
  • A sphere of land and sea and sky, A precious planet, where we thrive, Where mountains rise and oceans churn, And life in every form can learn.
  • The Earth is home to all we know, From forests green to deserts glow, From creatures small to giants tall, This planet harbors life for all.
  • The winds that blow, the rains that fall, The sun that warms, the moon that calls, The cycles of the seasons turn, In harmony, the Earth doth yearn.
  • Yet, we who live upon this sphere, Have caused it harm, with acts unclear, With thoughtless deeds and careless ways, We've put our planet in a daze.
  • But hope remains, for we can see, A brighter future yet to be, Where we, the caretakers of this place, Can heal the wounds and show our grace.
  • Let us protect this fragile sphere, For generations far and near, May they too see the beauty rare, Of our home, the Earth, suspended there.
